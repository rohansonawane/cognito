About Cognito

Cognito is a modern canvas app to draw, annotate, and ask AI to describe or solve what you sketch. It supports brushes, eraser, colors, undo/redo, image upload, and quick exports.

How to use

  • Choose a tool and color from the left panel. Adjust size with the slider.
  • Drag-and-drop an image onto the canvas to draw on top of it.
  • Use the zoom controls on the canvas. Reset view anytime.
  • Click Ask AI (bottom center) to analyze the current canvas. Optionally enter a prompt.
  • Save boards locally and download PNGs when you’re done.

Theme can be toggled from the header. All styling follows a unified blue palette for a clean, consistent feel.
